My Story

Full-Stack Web Developer | Angular, React, Node, & RESTful APIs   | Veteran

Determination and an eagerness to learn have been the keys to success in my career.

My story of how I got to this point is pretty unique. I’m a Veteran turned software developer.

I spent 7 years in the military as a member of the 19th Special Forces group. I had the privilege to be part of an amazing team full of incredibly smart and talented people, whose goal was to find solutions to complex problems around the world. During this period of my life I gained valuable skills, mental attributes, and years of real-world experience applying them.

During a particular training course, I learned the effective use of code and understand its importance when providing secure encryption on communication equipment. This experience was a pivotal moment in my life, as it sparked my desire to learn more about code and software development.

I decided to follow my desire, and jumped head first into learning to code. I found that my previous skills translated over into software perfectly. The hard work & dedication it takes to learn programming languages, high attention to detail to spot syntax errors, and ability to work effectively as a team. Upon leaving the military I was looking for the next challenge that would stretch my mind and present me with a whole new territory of learning. I found it in software development.
